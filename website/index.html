Fresco Contributors

Introduction

Welcome. Here you can find various applications and documents related to Fresco.

It is a place for people working on and with Fresco to share their work with others. Every such contributor has his own space here, for which he is responsible himself. This means that the resources here are not part of the official Fresco project and you use them on your own risk.

Instructions

If you want to check out the source, you can do that via CVS. You can view it with your browser via cvsweb. If there is enough demand, maybe we'll have snapshots as tarballs (via FTP or HTTP), too.

If you are a developer with some Fresco-related code or documents to share, please ask the admins for CVS write access, see the CVS policy.
